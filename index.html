Adding Math expressions

  1. Click on the "Add Math" button
  2. Enter the expressions where mentioned in blocks
  3. It is recommended to put different math expressions
    in different "Add Math" sections
  4. To close any box, press Esc (including this one)

Using Math expressions

  • It is recommended that you go through the basics of LaTeX from here: Link
  • Math expressions are categorised properly and the category names are listed as buttons on the toolbar
  • On clicking a category, various available expressions appear on the screen
  • Click on the expression that you want to enter; it will be added to your post at the position of the cursor
  • Fill in the gaps for constants or variables in the code that appears to get your desired expressions
  • If a space (" ") does not appear after clicking on "Check!", precede the space with a backslash "\"
  • Do not edit other parts of the code unless you have proper idea of what you are doing
  • The code that appears may be a little different from the actual Latex code for the expressions
To close any box of math symbols, press Esc
